### Notes
- ARC-GCI's stated removal (15-3-4-0.5-1-4.5 N-P-K-Ca-Mg-S kg/t) is the SA canonical line, repeated by GrainSA and FERTASA. **The 4.5 kg S/t is unusually high**; FAO/IFA 1992 gives 2.2 kg S/t. Surface as range, not single value.
- Plant N peaks before tassel; late-flowering N has diminishing returns and risks lodging. Pannar caps last side-dress at 25 kg N/ha post-flowering.
